About Me

I am Arpan Bhuva, a 3rd-year B.Tech ECE student at Dharmsinh Desai University, passionate about designing hardware that thinks - from transistor-level logic to intelligent embedded systems.

My core focus is VLSI and Chip Design - building digital systems from the ground up using Verilog HDL, implementing Finite State Machines, and verifying behaviour through simulation and synthesis on FPGA platforms.

Alongside VLSI, I work on Embedded Systems and IoT projects - bridging hardware and real-world applications through circuit design, sensor interfacing, and microcontroller-based prototyping.
